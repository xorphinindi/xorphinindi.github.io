Game Design

Mech Deck

My most ambitious card game. The idea is that you draft cards not into a deck, but a collection that represent your Mech. Then you create a deck and battle the other player. I have been slowly working on it over the past two-ish years and I like it a lot.

Ketu

Ketu is a small board game I made for my sister's birthday. She organized a white elephant gift exchange, so I made this game to participate.

Flappy Joust

Flappy Joust is a video game my cousin and I made. It got it's name because it mixes mechanics from flappy bird and joust. It is more a proof of concept rather than a whole game, but we wanted experience.

Shield Bearer

This game is a work in progress for a game design class. It works similarly to classic Zelda games.
